Pop Tale

Pop Tale is a group thesis project developed over almost a year by a team of six students. This animated short film gave me the opportunity to grow both technically and personally, working within a complete production pipeline from concept to final render. My main responsibilities included VFX, rendering and compositing, but I also provided support in 3D modeling, contributing to the creation of assets and environments.

Beyond the technical aspects, Pop Tale was a formative experience in teamwork, communication, and problem-solving, helping me develop a collaborative mindset and the ability to adapt to creative and technical challenges throughout production.
