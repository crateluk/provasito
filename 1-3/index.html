Robot

In this project, I focused on modeling and texturing a highly realistic robot, which was then integrated into live-action footage using Nuke. The work allowed me to refine my hard-surface modeling skills, an area I particularly enjoy, and to enhance my texturing techniques through Substance Painter.

also also developed my skilles in compositing, blending 3D renders with real-world footage to ensure accurate lighting, reflections, and shadows. Working on this project gave me the chance to follow a full cinematic pipeline, from initial asset creation to final integration, reinforcing my understanding of professional workflows.

 

 
 
 
 
 
 

The initial stage of the project focused on hard-surface modeling in Maya, aimed at achieving precise geometry and clean topology for a realistic robotic design. Subsequently, Substance 3D Painter was employed to develop textures across multiple UDIMs, following a PBR workflow.
