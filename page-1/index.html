About me

I am a recent graduate in CG Animation from IED Milan, where I had the opportunity to explore the technical and production aspects of computer graphics. During my studies, I developed a particular interest in visual effects, appreciating the precision and problem-solving involved in creating realistic and cohesive visuals.

Throughout my academic journey, I strengthened my technical foundation in 3D modeling, texturing, rendering, and compositing, gaining a solid understanding of the entire CG production pipeline. Working on various projects allowed me to apply these skills in practical contexts, optimizing workflows and ensuring consistency across different stages of production.

Using industry-standard software and collaborating within team-based projects helped me develop an organized, analytical, and detail-oriented approach to work. I value efficiency, accuracy, and collaboration in achieving high-quality results.

As I begin my professional career, I am eager to continue improving my technical expertise and contribute as a junior artist, supporting complex productions and learning from experienced professionals in the field.
